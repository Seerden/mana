# Timescales
These indicate how much time the user wishes there to be between reviews for terms of a given saturation level

## Example:
level | timescale 
:--- | :--- 
0	| 1 day 
1   | 3 days 
2	| 1 week 
3	| 2 weeks
4 	| 1 month

## Usage:
Combine these with a term's lastReviewed date to suggest reviews.
